Attend an event

Kings game? Concert? Check out the Golden 1 Center and Thunder Valley Casino for your entertainment.

Take in the culture

Check out the Crocker Art Museum or the CA Auto Museum, both located in downtown Sacramento.

Go to the lake

The Folsom Lake State Recreation Area is a great place to relax and enjoy nature, and visit the historic Black Miners Bar area.

Road Trip Friendly

Reno, Lake Tahoe, and the San Francisco Bay Area are less than three hours away by car, providing great opportunities for day/overnight trips. Other attractions in the area that are less than an hour drive include:

  • The Golfland Sunsplash Waterpark in Roseville
  • Six Flags Discovery Kingdom in Vallejo
  • The Vacaville Premium Outlets in Vacaville
  • Tours at the Jelly Belly Factory in Fairfield
